About Sunitha

Sunitha Prasad is a dedicated minister, educator, and counselor with a passion for building strong, god-centered families. As the Founder of Enrich Family Life (a part of Enrich Ministries), she brings over two decades of experience to her calling.

She specializes in teaching, preaching, and counseling in the critical areas of pre-marital preparation, marriage enrichment, and parenting. Alongside her husband, Dr. Sunny Prasad, she has been deeply involved in ministry for the last 20 years, combining academic rigor with practical, spiritual wisdom.
